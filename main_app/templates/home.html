Pokemon 5e Character Creator

This site was created for use with the Pokemon 5e Dungeons and Dragons setting created by @JOEtheDM. Currently it only supports inital creation of trainer characters and a selection of the starter pokemon and their information.
Pokémon5E is a homebrew system to D&D that layers the world of Pokémon on top of the core rules of D&D. If you have never played Dungeons & Dragons before, do not worry as the 5th edition is easy to get into! You do not need a lot to start playing: a few friends, the D&D Basic Rules, a couple of dice (or a dice app!) and of course the Pokémon5E supplement!
